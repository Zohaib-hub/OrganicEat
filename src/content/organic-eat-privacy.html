Privacy Policy

What information we collect

When you message us on WhatsApp to place an order, we collect your name, phone number, and delivery address. If you subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect your email address. We do not collect payment card details — all digital payments go through Easypaisa, JazzCash, or direct bank transfer.

How we use your information

We use your name, number, and address only to process and deliver your order. We use your phone number to send you order confirmations and delivery updates. If you join our WhatsApp broadcast, we use your number to send the daily menu update. We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not share your information with third parties outside of what's needed to complete your delivery.

WhatsApp broadcast list

When you join our WhatsApp broadcast list, you are opting in to receive one message per morning with the day's available menu. You can opt out at any time by messaging us and asking to be removed. We will remove you immediately.

To opt out of our WhatsApp broadcast at any time: message us and say "remove me from the broadcast list." Done within minutes.

Cookies and website data

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not track your browsing behaviour or collect analytics data that identifies you.

Data security

Your information is stored only on our WhatsApp and order records. We do not store payment information. We do not use third-party CRM software that stores your data on external servers.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you. You can ask us to delete your information at any time. Contact us on WhatsApp or email to do so and we'll handle it promptly.

Terms of Service

Orders

All orders are placed via WhatsApp. An order is confirmed only when we send you a WhatsApp confirmation message. We reserve the right to cancel an order if the item sells out before delivery and will notify you immediately if this happens.

Freshness and availability

All fresh food items are cooked daily in limited quantities. We cannot guarantee availability if you order after the batch has sold out. We will always inform you if an item is unavailable and offer you a place on the next day's list.

Delivery

Delivery is available across most of Lahore. Delivery charges vary by area and will be communicated at the time of order confirmation. We aim to deliver all orders within the estimated time communicated at confirmation. Delays due to traffic or weather will be communicated as soon as we are aware.

Payment

We accept cash on delivery and digital payment via Easypaisa, JazzCash, and bank transfer. Payment is due at the time of delivery (cash) or before delivery (digital).

Refunds and complaints

If you are unhappy with your order, message us on WhatsApp straight away. We handle every complaint personally and will always work to make it right. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis and we aim to resolve all issues fairly and quickly.

Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. The latest version will always be on this page with the update date shown at the top.

Questions about these policies?

Message us on WhatsApp or email us directly. We'll answer in plain language, not legal speak.
