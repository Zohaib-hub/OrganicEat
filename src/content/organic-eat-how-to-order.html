Step by step

Here's exactly what to do.

📱

Step 1 — Save our number first

WhatsApp only delivers messages from contacts. Save our number before you message — otherwise your order might not come through.

Step 2 — Send us a message

"Hi, I want to order [dish name] and my address is [your address]." That's all we need to get started. Even just "I want biryani" works — we'll ask for the rest.

🌶️

Step 3 — Tell us your preferences

For Biryani: mention your spice level — Mild, Medium, or Spicy. For Palak Murgh: no customisation needed. It comes exactly as it should.

Step 4 — We confirm everything

We'll reply with your total, delivery time, and payment details. Usually within a few minutes. No long waits.

Step 5 — Pay and your food arrives

Pay via cash on delivery or digital transfer. Your food arrives hot and sealed, usually within the estimated time we gave you.
