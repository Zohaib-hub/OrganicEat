Ordering

How do I place an order?+
WhatsApp us. Tell us what you want and your delivery address. We'll confirm everything within minutes. No app, no account, no checkout needed.
Do I need to create an account?+
No. No app, no account, no signup. Just WhatsApp.
Can I order in advance for tomorrow?+
Yes. Message us today and we'll note your order for tomorrow's batch. We'll confirm when it's ready and give you the delivery time.
Can I order multiple dishes at once?+
Yes. Just mention both in your message and we'll sort the quantity and total together.

Freshness & Food

Do you actually cook fresh every day?+
Every morning. One batch. Nothing is carried over from the day before. No frozen backup. Fresh only — that's the whole point.
What time is the food ready?+
By 10–11 AM every morning. Order early — limited portions go fast, especially biryani.
Do you use frozen chicken?+
Never. Fresh chicken, bought that morning, cooked the same day. This is something we don't compromise on.
Is there anything artificial in your food?+
No. Real ingredients only. No preservatives, no artificial flavouring, no colour, no fillers. What we say is in it — is in it.
Can I choose the spice level for Biryani?+
Yes. Mild, Medium, or Spicy. Just mention it when you order and we'll make sure it's right.
What if I have a food allergy?+
Message us before ordering. We'll tell you exactly what goes into each dish so you can make an informed choice. We never want to guess with this.

Delivery

Which areas do you deliver to?+
Most areas in Lahore. Send us your address on WhatsApp and we'll confirm in minutes. We go wherever our customers are.
Is delivery free?+
Delivery charges vary by distance. We'll give you the exact amount when we confirm your order. No surprises.
How long does delivery take?+
Depends on your area. We'll give you an estimated delivery time when we confirm your order and update you if anything changes.
What if my food arrives cold?+
That shouldn't happen — we pack carefully and deliver promptly. But if something is wrong, message us straight away and we'll make it right.

Payment

Not if you choose cash on delivery. For digital payment, we'll send you the details when we confirm your order.
