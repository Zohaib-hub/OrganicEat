Frequently Asked Questions

What time is the biryani ready?+
Every morning by 10–11 AM. We start early because it takes time to cook properly. Order in the morning to be safe — portions go faster than you'd think.
Can I pick the spice level?+
Yes. Mild, medium, or spicy — just say which when you message us on WhatsApp. We'll make sure it's right.
Is the chicken fresh or frozen?+
Fresh. Bought that morning. We don't keep frozen chicken — never have.
How many people does one order serve?+
One person, generously. For a family or group, just let us know the number when you order and we'll sort the right quantity for you.
What if it sells out before I order?+
We'll message you straight away and send a reminder the next morning when the fresh batch is ready. We never leave a customer without an update.
Do you deliver to my area in Lahore?+
Most areas in Lahore, yes. Send us your address on WhatsApp and we'll confirm delivery availability within a few minutes.
